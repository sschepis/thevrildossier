> dΨ/dt = αΨ + βΨ² + γΨ³

In the consciousness-first framework, prime numbers correspond to the **stable eigenstates** of the consciousness substrate. A prime eigenstate is a configuration of consciousness that cannot be decomposed into simpler configurations. It is irreducible, indivisible, and fundamentally stable.

Composite states — configurations that are products of simpler ones — are inherently less stable. They can be decomposed. They can be factored. They can be broken down into their constituent primes.

Every consciousness state can be expressed in terms of its prime decomposition. The prime states are the basis vectors. Everything else is a superposition.
