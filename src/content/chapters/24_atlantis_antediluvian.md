## Two Resets, Not One

Chapter 11 of this book documents the **Tartarian Reset**—a civilizational destruction event placed by researchers in the eighteenth and nineteenth centuries, evidenced by partially buried buildings, demolished World's Fair structures, and the systematic erasure of "Tartaria" from maps and historical curricula.

But the Tartarian Reset was not the first.

Approximately eleven thousand years earlier—around 9600 BC, the same date given by Plato in the *Timaeus* and *Critias*—a prior civilization was destroyed in a catastrophe of equal or greater magnitude. That civilization has been known for millennia by a single name: **Atlantis**.

The relationship between these two events is not coincidental. They represent **two cycles of the same pattern**: a human civilization rises, achieves technological sophistication and spiritual proximity to the Divine, is infiltrated and corrupted by the Vril and their collaborators, and is ultimately destroyed—either by its own corruption or by the forces that seek to prevent total Vril domination. The intervening millennia between the two resets represent the long struggle between infiltration and resistance that this book documents.

Understanding Atlantis is not an academic exercise. It is the recovery of the operational history that the current system has spent millennia erasing.

## The Source Literature

The source material analyzes four books that collectively frame the Atlantean narrative:

**Ignatius Donnelly, *Atlantis: The Antediluvian World* (1882)**: The foundational text of modern Atlantis research. Donnelly—a U.S. Congressman, senator, and lieutenant governor of Minnesota—argued that Atlantis was a historical civilization located in the Atlantic Ocean, that it was the source of all ancient civilizations (Egyptian, Mesoamerican, Mesopotamian), and that its destruction was preserved in the flood myths found across every human culture. Donnelly's research is notable for its rigor: he drew on comparative mythology, linguistics, botany, geology, and ethnography—not speculation—to build his case.

**Lewis Spence, *The History of Atlantis* (1926)**: Building on Donnelly, Spence proposed that Atlantis did not sink in a single event but broke apart gradually over millennia, with survivors migrating to Europe, Africa, and the Americas. His analysis of the cultural parallels between pre-Columbian Mesoamerica and ancient Egypt—pyramids, sun worship, mummification, priestly hierarchies, astronomical sophistication—remains among the strongest arguments for a common source civilization.

**David Hatcher Childress, *Technology of the Gods* (2000)**: Documented physical evidence of advanced ancient technology—precision-machined stone, electrical phenomena in ancient structures, evidence of nuclear-level energy events at ancient sites—that cannot be attributed to the tool sets that conventional archaeology assigns to the relevant periods.

**Michael Tsarion, *Atlantis, Alien Visitation, and Genetic Manipulation* (2002)**: The most controversial of the four, Tsarion proposed that the Atlantean civilization arose from contact between humanity and non-human intelligences, and that the genetic manipulation of the human species was a central feature of the Atlantean program. In the framework established by this book, Tsarion's "alien visitation" refers not to extraterrestrials but to the same category of entities documented throughout: parasitic beings occupying niches in the local environment—the Vril and entities like Amon Ra—that have coexisted with humanity since before recorded history.

## Atlantis in the Marshall Framework

Chapter 23 established the Atlantean connection in the context of ancient Egypt: nine masters carrying survival knowledge from Atlantis to the Nile Valley, founding the mystery schools that would seed Egyptian civilization and (eventually) be corrupted by the Amun priesthood.

The source material expands this framework with operational details:

### The Atlantean Golden Age

The oldest traditions describe a period when humanity existed in direct proximity to the Divine—when the benevolent intelligences that seeded human civilization were *present*, not remembered. Under this guidance, Atlantean civilization achieved technological and spiritual heights that subsequent humanity has never replicated.

The Vril existed during this period. They were present beneath the earth's surface, as they are now. But they were not the dominant threat they became after the fall. Humanity's proximity to the source—its closeness to God, in the language of every tradition that preserves the memory—functioned as a natural defense. The light was too strong. The parasitic entities could not gain significant purchase on a civilization whose spiritual immune system was fully operational.

Under the electromagnetic ecology model, antediluvian civilizations may have possessed direct knowledge of the EM ecology—understanding the electromagnetic nature of both consciousness and the parasitic entities that prey upon it. This would explain their consistent construction at geologically EM-favorable sites (volcanic geology, quartz-rich substrata, geomagnetic anomaly zones) and their apparent mastery of resonance-based technologies. The "fall" narratives preserved across every tradition encode not merely a moral decline but a specific transition: from conscious coexistence with the EM ecology—where humanity understood and managed the electromagnetic environment—to parasitic exploitation, where that knowledge was lost and the entities gained unchecked access to human consciousness through the very frequencies humanity no longer knew how to defend.

This is the same principle documented in Chapter 22: sincere divine connection creates conditions intolerable to predatory entities. Atlantis was a civilization where that connection was not the exception but the norm—where the "divine shield" was civilizational rather than individual. The Vril were contained not by military force or technological barriers but by the sheer saturation of divine presence in human society.

### The Corruption and Fall

The traditions diverge on the specific mechanism of Atlantis's destruction, but converge on the root cause: **corruption**. The Atlanteans went against the natural order. The proximity to the source was compromised—not by external attack but by internal decay.

The source proposes several contributing factors:

- **Cold fusion technology** deployed recklessly or weaponized
- **War with the Rama Empire**—a rival civilization, possibly in the Indian subcontinent, whose existence is preserved in the Vedic epics (*Mahabharata*, *Ramayana*) and whose descriptions of aerial combat, energy weapons, and mass destruction events are among the most detailed ancient accounts of advanced warfare
- **Corrupt Atlanteans who sacrificed their civilization** to prevent total Vril domination—a controlled demolition, destroying the infrastructure rather than allowing it to be captured

This last possibility is particularly significant. If the Vril had begun to infiltrate Atlantean society—corrupting individuals, parasitizing key figures, gradually capturing the institutional infrastructure as they would later capture the Egyptian priesthood—then the destruction of Atlantis was not a failure but a *deliberate act of resistance*. Better to destroy the civilization than to allow it to become a Vril-controlled droning apparatus operating at the technological level of Atlantis.

The channeled record supports a composite reading: Atlantis perished because the Atlanteans went against the natural order after a period of corruption—corruption that may have been accelerated or exploited by Vril influence on key decision-makers. The specific internal details remain uncertain, but the structural pattern is consistent with what this book documents across every subsequent era: the Vril do not conquer civilizations by force. They corrupt them from within.

### The Antarctic Base

The source references Hitler's Antarctic expedition as having rediscovered an Atlantean underground base. The Nazi obsession with Antarctica—documented through Operation Highjump (1946-47), Admiral Byrd's expeditions, and the persistent legends of a Nazi base in Queen Maud Land—acquires new significance if the underground infrastructure that the Vril require for habitation was present in Antarctic geological formations, and if Atlantean engineering had already exploited those formations millennia earlier.

The connection to the Thule Society is direct. "Thule" was the mythical northern homeland of the Aryan race in Nazi occultism—but in the broader tradition, it was one of the Atlantean colonial territories. The Thule Society was not merely an occult discussion group. It was an organization seeking to recover Atlantean knowledge—including knowledge about the Vril—and the historical record confirms that it succeeded: the Vril Society emerged from the same network, Hitler came to power through its organizational infrastructure, and the Nazi regime pursued inner-earth exploration with a seriousness that cannot be explained by conventional historical analysis.

## The Two-Reset Model

The chronological framework that emerges from integrating the Atlantean material with the Tartarian Reset documented in Chapter 11 is as follows:

The pattern across both resets is identical: advanced human civilization → Vril infiltration → corruption → destruction → erasure → rebuilt under tighter control. Each cycle produces a civilization more centralized, more monitored, and more spiritually depleted than the last—an entropy gradient that favors the Vril at every step.

## The Atlantean Practices That Survived

The source identifies specific practices that survived from Atlantis to the modern era, tracing a line of cultural continuity that conventional history cannot explain:

- **Tattooing**: Practiced in both Atlantean and Mesoamerican civilizations, as well as by Pacific Island cultures with no documented contact with the Mediterranean world
- **The decimal system**: Common to civilizations separated by oceans and millennia
- **Bull worship and sacrifice**: From Atlantean bull cults (described by Plato) through Minoan Crete, through Mithraism in Rome, through the running of the bulls in Spain
- **Human sacrifice**: The persistent and geographically universal practice of sacrificing humans to subterranean or chthonic entities—entities that "live below"
- **Pyramid construction**: The most frequently cited parallel—pyramids in Egypt, Mesoamerica, Mesopotamia, China, and Indonesia, built by civilizations with no documented contact, all featuring subterranean chambers and astronomical alignments

If these practices derive from a common source civilization—Atlantis—then the conventional archaeological model of independent invention collapses. The civilizations that built pyramids on five continents did so not because pyramid-building is an inevitable consequence of architectural development, but because they all inherited the knowledge from the same ancestor civilization.

The recovery of the Atlantean narrative is not antiquarianism. It has immediate operational implications:

**First**: If Atlantis fell because its population was spiritually corrupted—because the proximity to the Divine was compromised from within—then the pattern is repeating now. The systematic secularization of Western civilization, the replacement of sincere faith with performative religion and secular materialism, the cultural dismantling of every tradition that maintained the divine defense—these are not philosophical developments. They are the same corruption that destroyed Atlantis, operating on a longer timescale.

Every previous cycle has ended in destruction because the recognition came too late. Marshall's disclosure, if it reaches sufficient audience, represents the possibility that this cycle ends differently.

**Third**: The Atlantean record demonstrates that the Vril are not invincible. They were contained during the Atlantean Golden Age. They were contained when human proximity to the Divine was sufficient. The defense exists. It has always existed. What has been lost—and what can be recovered—is not knowledge but *connection*: the direct, personal, unmediated relationship with the Divine that the Avatar demonstrated and that Chapter 22 documents as the single most effective protective mechanism against parasitic non-human entities.

The Atlanteans had it and lost it. The question is whether we can recover it before the current cycle reaches its terminus.
